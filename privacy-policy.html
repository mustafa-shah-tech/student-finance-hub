Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to PakStudentFinance. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any personal data you share with us. This Privacy Policy outlines what information we collect, how it is used, and how we keep it safe. By using our website, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We believe in data minimization. We do not require you to create an account, nor do we store any of the personal data you enter into our calculators (like your CGPA, degree level, or loan details) on our servers. All calculations happen locally in your browser.

  • Information you provide voluntarily: If you sign up for deadline email alerts or fill out our feedback form, we collect your name and email address.
  • Automatically collected data: We use Google Analytics to monitor website traffic. This may collect anonymous data including your IP address, browser type, device information, and pages visited.

3. How We Use Your Information

Any information we collect is used strictly for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our services.
  • To send you the email alerts you specifically requested regarding scholarship deadlines.
  • To analyze website usage statistics in order to improve user experience.
  • To serve personalized advertisements via Google AdSense.

4. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services that may place cookies on your browser or collect information about your visit:

  • Google Analytics: We use Google Analytics to track and analyze site traffic. Google may use this data to contextualize and personalize the ads of its own advertising network.
  • Google AdSense: Third party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to our sites and/or other sites on the Internet. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.
  • Mailchimp / Email Services: If you subscribe to our newsletter, your email address is securely processed and stored by our email delivery partner. We do not sell your email address to anyone.

5. User Rights and Data Deletion

You have the right to request access to the personal data we hold about you, or ask that we delete it. Because we only store email addresses explicitly given for communication, you can opt-out at any time by clicking the "Unsubscribe" link at the bottom of any email alert we send you, or by contacting us.

6. Cookies

A cookie is a small piece of text sent to your browser by a website you visit. We use cookies primarily for tracking website performance and serving advertisements through Google AdSense. You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent.

7. Contact Information

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@pakstudentfinance.com
